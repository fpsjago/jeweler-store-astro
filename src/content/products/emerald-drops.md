### Care Instructions

Emeralds require special care due to their natural characteristics:

- **Daily Wear**: Remove before washing hands, applying lotion, or physical activities
- **Cleaning**: Use only lukewarm water and mild soap; pat dry gently
- **Storage**: Store separately in soft pouch to prevent scratches
- **Avoid**: Ultrasonic cleaners, steam cleaning, harsh chemicals, sudden temperature changes
- **Professional Care**: Annual inspection recommended

### Emerald Enhancement Note

Like most emeralds worldwide, these stones have received minor clarity enhancement (oiling), a traditional and accepted practice dating back centuries. This enhancement is stable with proper care and does not affect long-term beauty or value.
