## Bespoke Design Service

Transform your vision into reality with our exclusive bespoke design service. Work directly with our master jewelers to create a truly one-of-a-kind piece that captures your unique story, celebrates a special moment, or realizes a dream you've held for years.

### Why Choose Bespoke?

**Uniqueness:**
No one else will ever wear your design. It's singular, personal, and exclusively yours.

**Perfect Fit:**
Every aspect is tailored to your preferences – size, style, materials, budget.

**Meaningful:**
Incorporate personal symbols, family stones, or design elements that tell your story.

**Investment:**
Custom pieces often appreciate more than mass-produced jewelry due to their uniqueness and craftsmanship.

### Our Process: From Dream to Reality

#### Phase 1: Discovery Consultation (1-2 hours)

**Getting to Know You:**
- Share your vision, inspiration, and ideas
- Discuss occasion, meaning, and intended wearer
- Review reference images or existing pieces
- Explore style preferences

**Technical Discussion:**
- Budget parameters and timeline
- Material options (metals, gemstones)
- Size and wearability considerations
- Care and maintenance expectations

**Outcome:**
Clear understanding of your desires and a roadmap for the design process.

#### Phase 2: Design Development (2-4 weeks)

**Initial Sketches:**
- Our designer creates multiple concept sketches
- Range of approaches exploring your vision
- Hand-drawn illustrations with notes
- Present 3-5 distinct concepts

**Revision & Refinement:**
- Select favorite direction
- Refine proportions, details, and style
- Adjust elements to perfect the design
- Collaborate until you're completely satisfied

**3D CAD Modeling:**
- Create precise 3D computer model
- View from all angles
- Make final adjustments
- See realistic rendering before production

**Outcome:**
Finalized design with your approval and a detailed rendering of the finished piece.

#### Phase 3: Material Selection (1 week)

**Metal Selection:**
- Choose from platinum, 18K gold (yellow, white, rose), or mixed metals
- Discuss properties and appearance
- Consider durability and maintenance
- Review pricing for each option

**Gemstone Selection:**
- We source stones specifically for your piece
- Present multiple options meeting your criteria
- Evaluate color, clarity, size, and origin
- Show stones in person when possible
- GIA or laboratory certificates for all stones

**Final Quote:**
- Detailed cost breakdown
- Materials, labor, and timelines
- Payment schedule (typical: 50% deposit, 50% on completion)
- Written agreement protecting both parties

**Outcome:**
Selected materials with certificates and final approved quote.

#### Phase 4: Crafting (4-8 weeks)

**Metal Fabrication:**
- Cast or forge the base structure
- Hand-finish all surfaces
- Create settings for gemstones
- Add decorative elements

**Stone Setting:**
- Precision-set each gemstone
- Master setter ensures perfect alignment
- Secure prong or bezel work
- Quality check each setting

**Finishing Work:**
- Hand-polish to perfection
- Apply texture or patina if desired
- Add engraving or personalization
- Final quality inspection

**Progress Updates:**
- Weekly photo updates
- Opportunity to visit workshop (by appointment)
- Address any concerns immediately
- Ensure you're excited about progress

**Outcome:**
Your bespoke piece, crafted to perfection by master artisans.

#### Phase 5: Presentation & Delivery

**Final Reveal:**
- In-person presentation appointment
- Detailed viewing with explanation of techniques
- Ensure perfect fit and comfort
- Make any final minor adjustments

**Documentation:**
- Certificate of authenticity
- Detailed description of materials and techniques
- Photographs of creation process
- Gemstone certificates
- Insurance appraisal

**Care Instructions:**
- Personalized care guide for your specific piece
- Recommendations for cleaning and storage
- Schedule for professional maintenance
- Warranty and service information

**Outcome:**
Your completed bespoke jewel, ready to wear and cherish for a lifetime.

### Pricing & Timeline

**Typical Investment Ranges:**

**Simple Pieces:**
- $2,000 - $5,000
- Basic designs with modest gemstones
- 4-6 week timeline
- Examples: simple rings, studs, pendants

**Medium Complexity:**
- $5,000 - $15,000
- Multiple gemstones or intricate metalwork
- 6-8 week timeline
- Examples: engagement rings, necklaces, bracelets

**Complex Pieces:**
- $15,000 - $50,000
- Significant gemstones, extensive hand-work
- 8-12 week timeline
- Examples: major cocktail rings, statement necklaces, suites

**Heirloom Major Projects:**
- $50,000+
- Museum-quality craftsmanship
- 12-16 week timeline
- Examples: parure sets, major gemstone pieces

**Factors Affecting Price:**
- Gemstone cost (often 40-60% of total)
- Metal choice and weight
- Design complexity and handwork hours
- Setting difficulty
- Special techniques (engraving, enameling, etc.)

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much does custom jewelry cost?**
A: It varies dramatically based on materials and complexity. Simple pieces start around $2,000, while elaborate designs can exceed $50,000. During consultation, we'll provide detailed estimates based on your specific vision.

**Q: How long does the process take?**
A: Typical timeline is 6-10 weeks from design approval to completion. Rush service available for engagements and special occasions.

**Q: Can you work with my budget?**
A: Yes. We'll design to your budget constraints, suggesting material choices and design approaches that maximize beauty within your parameters.

**Q: What if I have heirloom stones to incorporate?**
A: Perfect! Bringing new life to family stones is one of our specialties. We'll assess the stones, discuss design options, and create something that honors the past while looking to the future.

**Q: Can I make changes during the process?**
A: Minor changes are possible early in the process. Once production begins, significant changes may require starting over (with cost implications). That's why we're so thorough during the design phase.

**Q: What if I'm not happy with the result?**
A: In 15 years, we've never had a client unsatisfied with their bespoke piece because of our thorough approval process. However, we stand behind our work and will make it right if anything is less than perfect.
